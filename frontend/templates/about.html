About BioTree

BioTree is a powerful and intuitive web application built with Go, JavaScript, HTML, and CSS, that allows users to upload documents in .pdf, .txt, or .docx format and generates dynamic SVG visualizations based on the content.

Our Mission

Whether you're analyzing biological data, scientific text, or general structured information, BioTree offers a fast, clean, and interactive way to visualize it. Our mission is to make data visualization accessible and straightforward for researchers, students, and professionals.
